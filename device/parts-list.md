# Parts List

## MVP Parts

- Arduino UNO Q 2GB
- Arduino Nicla Vision
- USB-C power bank or LiPo battery system
- small OLED or e-paper display
- record button
- consent switch
- mark/flag button
- power switch
- status LEDs
- vibration motor
- buzzer or speaker
- optional GPS module
- optional RTC module
- enclosure
- wrist strap or lanyard

## Instrumentation Revision

The "optional GPS module" line above is formalized and extended (multi-constellation/multi-band receiver, external antenna, magnetometer, pressure sensor) by `docs/mvp/hardware/instrumentation-revision-v0.02.md`, written to support the Photographic and Cartographic Witness Layers (`schemas/photographic-witness.schema.json`, `schemas/location-evidence.schema.json`, and related schemas). This list is not rewritten in place; the revision document is the authoritative companion for what changed and why.
