# AI Permissions

## Governing Rule

SSL-005 — AI Is Assistive: AI may suggest, summarize, transcribe, translate, classify, or draft. AI may not publish, erase, override, or govern.

## AI May

- produce a transcript draft
- produce a translation draft
- produce an alt text draft
- suggest entities
- suggest risk flags
- produce a summary draft

## AI May Not

- override consent
- publish
- delete evidence
- erase provenance
- finalize risk
- decide public status
