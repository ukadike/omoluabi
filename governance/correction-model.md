# Correction Model

## Governing Rule

SSL-007 — Correction Visibility: corrections must preserve what changed, when, why, and by whom.

This rule operationalizes Constitutional Principle #8: corrections remain visible (`governance/principles.md`).

## Required Fields

A correction record must capture:

- the field or claim being corrected
- the prior value, preserved (not deleted)
- the new value
- who made the correction
- when the correction was made
- why the correction was made

## Why This Matters

Silent edits erase accountability and make it impossible for readers, subjects, or reviewers to know what changed. Omoluabi treats a correction as a new, linked record rather than an overwrite.
