# Principles

## One Sentence

Omoluabi is governed observation infrastructure: every record must carry consent, provenance, risk, accessibility, publication status, and review history.

## Constitutional Principles

1. Every record begins as an observation.
2. Every observation has provenance.
3. Consent is versioned.
4. Accessibility metadata is required before publication.
5. Risk is assessed before exposure.
6. AI may assist but cannot govern.
7. Human review is required for publication.
8. Corrections remain visible.
9. Uncertainty must be represented.
10. Communities retain agency.
11. Private is a valid archival state.
12. Public does not mean context-free.
13. APIs expose governed records, not raw extraction.
