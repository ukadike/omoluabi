# ADR-0002: Governance Before AI

**Status:** accepted

## Decision

Governance rules must be deterministic, inspectable, and human-reviewable. AI may suggest classifications but cannot publish, erase, override consent, or finalize risk.

## Why

Public trust requires accountability and reviewability.

## Consequences

More upfront schema work, but greater transparency and funder trust.
