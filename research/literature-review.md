## Areas to Expand

### Civic Technology
Study public-interest systems, community-owned infrastructure, participatory design, anti-extractive technology, and civic data stewardship.

### Journalism and Editorial Systems
Study editorial workflows, corrections, source validation, story provenance, evidence ladders, public accountability, and news archives.

### Archival Science
Study digital preservation, metadata standards, provenance, community archives, oral history protocols, and long-term access.

### Accessibility
Study WCAG, WAI-ARIA, blind-editor workflows, captions, transcripts, alt text, tactile/haptic interfaces, plain language, keyboard-first interaction, and low-bandwidth access.

### Responsible AI
Study human-in-the-loop AI, explainability, uncertainty, bias, model permissions, risk assessment, AI audit trails, and governance separation.

### Embedded AI / TinyML
Study edge sensing, Arduino AI boards, TinyML, local classification, sensor fusion, low-power operation, and offline workflows.

### Local-First Software
Study local storage, offline-first sync, conflict resolution, CRDTs, data ownership, and low-connectivity resilience.

### APIs and Federation
Study OpenAPI, versioning, schema registries, public archives, interoperability, and trust-preserving data exchange.
