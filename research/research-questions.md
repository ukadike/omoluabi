# Research Questions

## Research Position

Omoluabi sits at the intersection of:

- civic technology
- journalism infrastructure
- archival science
- accessibility
- responsible AI
- human-computer interaction
- local-first software
- digital preservation
- embedded sensing
- TinyML and edge AI
- public knowledge systems
- indigenous and community knowledge governance

## Core Research Questions

1. What is the smallest trustworthy unit of public observation?
2. How can governance be embedded before publication rather than after harm?
3. How can accessibility metadata become required infrastructure rather than optional remediation?
4. How can AI assist observation without becoming the governing authority?
5. How can field devices preserve consent, provenance, and risk context offline?
6. How can public knowledge systems remain legible to humans while becoming API-ready for machines?
7. How can one schema system support journalism, environmental sensing, oral history, food archives, speculative research, and community memory?
