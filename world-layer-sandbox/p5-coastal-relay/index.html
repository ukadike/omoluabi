UMADA · World-layer sandbox · Not canon

Coastal Relay

A painted mood study of the Umada coast at dusk: weather moving over the water and a relay of light travelling along the horizon — four quiet ticks and then a bloom. It is a felt echo of the opening rhythm and of the idea of a relay/signal system, not a depiction of any sign or place.

This page is exploratory. It asserts no canon, depicts no Nago signs or glyphs, names no real-world location, and is deliberately separate from the archival presentation layer (which stays monochrome and motion-free). If your system requests reduced motion, the scene paints a single still frame.

Built with p5.js (vendored locally under vendor/p5.min.js, LGPL-2.1 — see vendor/p5-LICENSE.txt). No external network calls.
